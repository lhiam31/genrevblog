San Roque Parish

This is the oldest church here in cordova, But before the structure that you now see was built, the residents of the town built a rickety structure made of stones, wood harigues or tabique pampango and a roof of nipa palm, church records said. It was built under Fr. Jose Salazar, the first parish priest. On May 22, 1863, the Cordoba Parish was presented to the Cebu District and was designated San Roque Parish of Cordoba, proclaiming Sr. San Roque as the patron saint.

CORDOVA LANTAW

Lantaw is a Bisaya word that translates to “look out”. It is a floating restaurant located in Day-as, Cordova where you can enjoy having an intimate dinner with your family while watching the sun set in the horizon with the calming sound of seawater surrounding the restaurant. I would eat here if I have a money but it's pricey here so let's go to the next place# with my bike

CORDOVA 10,000 ROSES

This café is famous for being the first in the Philippines to feature thousands of LED-powered roses. The best time to go is before sunset until closing time, when the roses are glowing. From here, you can see the skyline of Cebu City and its neighboring cities and municipalities. 10,000 RosesThis tourist spot got destroyed by typhoon oddette but now it's back on business.
Cafe is located in Day-as Barangay Rd, Cordova, 6017 Cebu, alongside Lantaw Floating Restaurant.

You can take a picture here with your families or friends etc.
